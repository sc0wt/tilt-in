Flicker tonic began as a proposed installation inspired by the work of the French op-art collective, GRAV. The result of an initial material collection from TILT, the joint practice of Lily Clark and Tristan Scow.The two designers share an interest in shaping meditative environments through objects that both produce and manipulate light, relying on a viewer to complete each piece through careful, sustained looking.
