Flicker Tonic is the debut collection from TILT, the joint practice of Lily Clark and Tristan Scow. The two designers share an interest in shaping meditative environments through objects that both produce and interact with light. Flicker Tonic relies on the viewer to complete each piece through careful, sustained looking. With the help of automated processes, the laser cut mobiles, lamps, and programable blinds magnify subtle interactions between light and shadow.
